Pro Tips

Fitness Golden Rules

Simple habits that separate results from excuses

Sleep 7-9 Hours

Muscle is built during sleep, not in the gym. Growth hormone peaks at night - never skip recovery.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase weight or reps every week. Your muscles need new challenges to grow stronger.

Stay Hydrated

Even mild dehydration drops performance by 10-20%. Keep a water bottle with you at all times.

Protein Every Meal

Aim for 1.6-2.2g protein per kg of bodyweight daily, spread across all your meals evenly.

Consistency Beats Intensity

Showing up daily beats one extreme workout per month. Build the habit first, results follow.

Mind-Muscle Connection

Focus on the muscle you are training. Slow controlled reps with intent beat sloppy heavy lifts.
